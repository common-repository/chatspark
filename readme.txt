Embed ChatSpark chatbot on your WordPress site.

== Description ==

Dive into the world of omnichannel AI chatbots and boost your customer interactions across all platforms! Website? Check! Social media? Absolutely! Even messaging apps like WhatsApp and Slack? You bet! Say goodbye to the 9-5 constraints and hello to 24/7 efficient customer support. Let's supercharge your engagement and save on those staffing costs. Elevate your customer experience with ChatSpark today!

The ChatSpark plugin allows ChatSpark customers to easily integrate their chatbots into their WordPress sites. Simply install and activate the plugin, enter your Chatbot ID in the plugin settings panel, and you're all set.

Note: A ChatSpark account and an active subscription are required to obtain a Chatbot ID and use this plugin. Visit [ChatSpark.io](https://chatspark.io/) to sign up and explore pricing plans.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `chatspark` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. After activation, you'll see a menu named ChatSpark on the left side of the WordPress admin dashboard.
4. Click on it, enter your Chatbot ID, and save the changes.
5. The chatbot will now be embedded on your site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I find my ChatSpark Chatbot ID? =

You can find your chatbot ID by logging into [ChatSpark.io](https://chatspark.io/login), going to My AI Chatbots > find your Chatbot > copy the ID.
